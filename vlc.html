VLC Media Player

Introduction

VLC Media Player is a free and open-source multimedia player developed by VideoLAN. It is one of the most versatile media players, capable of playing almost any video or audio format without the need for extra codecs. VLC is lightweight, fast, and works across all major platforms.

History

VLC started in 1996 as a student project called VideoLAN at École Centrale Paris. Initially designed to stream video across a campus network, it later evolved into a full media player. Today, it is one of the most widely used open-source applications globally.

Features

  • Plays all major video and audio formats
  • Supports streaming protocols
  • Lightweight and fast
  • Cross-platform compatibility
  • Completely free with no ads or spyware

How to Download

VLC can be downloaded for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. It is regularly updated by the VideoLAN community and comes with built-in codecs for smooth playback.
